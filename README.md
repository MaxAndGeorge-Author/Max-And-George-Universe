# Max-And-George-Universe

Welcome to the documentation of the Max & George Universe, home to the symbiotic Xorakion organism, the transformed Arcaxhir people, the Xorathian civilization, and their unique language, Arcaxh.

## About This Project
This repository contains the public documentation of the Max & George Universe, including:
- The Arcaxh language and its cultural significance
- Xorakion biology and its effects on host organisms
- The history and culture of the Arcaxhir people
- Key locations and characters

## Navigation
The documentation is organized by topic. Major sections include:
- Language Guide
- Biology & Science
- History & Culture
- Characters & Places
